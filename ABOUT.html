Who We Are?

is a Egyptian online fast fashion retailer. It was founded in 2021 by Farah Ahmed, Egypt . The company is known for its affordably priced apparel. In its early stages, ZEBRA was considered more of a drop shipping business than a retailer. Currently the company is not involved in clothing design and manufacturing, and instead obtains its products from the wholesale clothing market in China.
